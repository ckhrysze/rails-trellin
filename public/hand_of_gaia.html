One with the Beasts
1st circle: Form of the Beast
One form per circle. Weight must be the same. Standard action. Will save everyday, days in form to not become the form. All rolls and physical stats as that animal.
2nd circle: Talks with Animals
Can talk freely with animals.
Choose a known form, and a track whose primary stat matches the primary stat of that form (dex for feline, str for bear, etc). BaB and track abilities as they make sense.
